Small team. Sharp edges.

Sunstar Macedon is a tech studio founded in 2025. We do three things really well — and we do them like a mosaic: one deliberate tile at a time.

Who we are

A studio built around proof.

We're a small but sharp team that finds the cracks before your users — or attackers — do, writes clean code that ships on time, and renders 3D that actually looks the part. No bloated teams. No hand-holding.

That focus is the point. Because we're small, the people scoping your project are the people building and testing it. Nothing gets lost in a handoff, and nothing ships that we wouldn't run ourselves.

The Sunstar way

A mosaic is only as strong as its smallest tile.

The Vergina sun — our namesake — was set one tessera at a time. Every piece deliberate, every joint tight, the whole thing stronger than any single stone. We build software the same way: small surface area, sharp attention, and a finish that holds up when people look closely.
