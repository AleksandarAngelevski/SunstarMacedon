Questions, answered

Frequently asked.

What founders and security-conscious teams ask before they hire a software testing, development, and 3D studio.

Q1What does Sunstar Macedon do?

We're a small tech studio focused on three things: testing & security (QA, penetration testing, security audits, and compliance), software development (web, mobile, APIs, and automation), and 3D design & animation (models, product renders, and animation).

Q2Can you help us get SOC 2 or ISO 27001 ready?

Yes. We guide teams through compliance frameworks including SOC 2, ISO 27001, GDPR, HIPAA, and PCI DSS, and harden your product so your security holds up on paper and in practice.

Q3Do you offer penetration testing and security audits?

We do. We run thorough QA and penetration testing plus security audits to find bugs and vulnerabilities before your users — or attackers — do.

Q4What kind of software do you build?

Clean, reliable code for web apps, mobile apps, ecommerce stores, APIs, automation tools, and AI integrations — delivered on time without a bloated team.

Q5Who do you work with?

Startups and founders who need software built, and security-conscious teams who need compliance and penetration testing. We focus on small and medium businesses.

Q6How do I start a project?

Book a call through our Upwork profile. Tell us the problem and we'll scope the right mix of testing, development, and 3D.
