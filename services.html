Overview

A full-stack studio for shipping safely.

Sunstar Macedon pairs software testing and security with development and 3D design, so the thing you ship is fast, safe, and looks the part. We find bugs and vulnerabilities through QA and penetration testing, prove your security against frameworks like SOC 2, ISO 27001, GDPR, HIPAA, and PCI DSS, write clean code for web, mobile, and ecommerce, and produce 3D models, renders, and animation. No bloated teams — just the specialists your project needs.
